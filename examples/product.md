# Thornwood Contract Furniture

## What We Do

We design and manufacture contract-grade sofas and seating for hospitality, healthcare, and corporate clients. Our focus is on durability, design flexibility, and our proprietary EasyRest sleeper system.

## Key Capabilities

### EasyRest Sleeper System
- Patented fold-out mechanism that doesn't store mattress inside the sofa
- Uses a separate 6" memory foam mattress that stores in closet or under bed
- No springs, no jamming, no caught linens
- Mechanism rated for 10,000+ cycles
- Guests report 40% higher comfort vs traditional sleeper sofas

### Contract-Grade Construction
- Hardwood frames with reinforced joints (lifetime warranty)
- High-density foam rated for 100,000+ compression cycles
- All fabrics meet CAL 117-2013 fire standards by default
- Optional CAL 133 compliance for healthcare settings
- Fabrics tested for 100,000+ double rubs (Wyzenbeek method)

### Design Flexibility
- 8 base silhouettes from mid-century to contemporary
- 200+ commercial-grade fabrics in stock
- Custom fabric programs available (6-week lead time for COM)
- Mix-and-match: different fabrics for body, cushions, and piping
- Private labeling available

### White Glove Logistics
- In-house delivery teams in 23 metro areas
- Old furniture removal and recycling included
- Installation and inspection on-site
- Single point of contact from order to completion
